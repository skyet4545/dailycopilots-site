### Layout

   - Compass avatar

## Onboarding Flow (5 Screens)

### Screen 1: Welcome
- **Icon:** Compass (large)
- **Title:** "Welcome to" + "Bible Copilot" (accent color)
- **Subtitle:** "Your AI Bible study companion. Go deeper into Scripture with trusted, grounded insights."
- **Features:**
  - 📖 Study any verse with guided methods
  - 🎯 Get insights grounded in Scripture
  - 💬 Ask follow-up questions anytime
- **Button:** "Get Started"

### Screen 2: Not Just Any AI
- **Icon:** 🛡️
- **Title:** "Not Just Any AI"
- **Description:** Trust case — grounded in biblical truth, not speculation
- **Comparison:**
  - ❌ ChatGPT: "Many scholars believe..."
  - ✓ Bible Copilot: "Scripture teaches..."

### Screen 3: The Method Matters
- **Icon:** Compass
- **Title:** "The Method Matters"
- **Description:** Inductive Bible study method
- **Mode Display:** 3 on top row, 2 on bottom row

### Screen 4: Go Deep
- **Icon:** 💬
- **Title:** "Go Deep, Not Wide"
- **Description:** Follow-up questions, word studies
- **Examples:**
  - "What does 'predestined' mean?"
  - "How does this relate to free will?"
  - "Show me cross-references"

### Screen 5: Ready to Study
- **Icon:** ✨
- **Title:** "Ready to Study"
- **Description:** "Enter any verse and let your AI Bible study companion guide you deeper into God's Word."
- **Show:** Sample search input
- **Button:** "Start Studying" (green)
