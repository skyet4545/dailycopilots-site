### F. Wikipedia — NOT YET ELIGIBLE

Bible Copilot does **not** meet Wikipedia's notability bar (`WP:GNG`) today. Do not attempt creation; pages get speedy-deleted and the brand gets flagged.

**Eligibility criteria** (revisit when all three are true):
1. **3+ independent reliable sources** with substantial coverage (not press releases, not affiliate listicles). Trade press (TechCrunch, Christianity Today, Gospel Coalition feature, Religion News Service) counts. Self-published blogs do not.
2. Coverage spans **at least 6 months** (one-week launch coverage isn't durable notability).
3. Sources are **about Bible Copilot specifically**, not just listing it among 14 other apps.

Realistic earliest eligibility: **Q4 2026** if Tim Challies + ChurchTech Today + one trade outlet all cover the app in 2026. Until then, owned-property + directory listings are the play.
