Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Bible Copilot ("the App") is developed by Carlos Reyes. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All Bible study data (saved passages, journal entries, reading plan progress) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

AI-Powered Study

When you use the AI study feature, your Bible verse queries are sent to our server to generate study responses. These queries are not stored, logged, or associated with your identity. No account or login is required.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

Analytics

The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate user accounts, databases, or cloud storage for user content. If you delete the App, all local data is removed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@scripturecopilot.com.
